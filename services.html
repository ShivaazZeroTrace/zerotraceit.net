Our Capabilities

Comprehensive ITAD Solutions

Professional, secure, and environmentally responsible disposal for all your decommissioned IT hardware.

IT Asset Pickup and Logistics

We coordinate the entire pickup process of decommissioned IT equipment directly from your location. Our professional team handles all logistics, removing the burden from your internal staff while maintaining a secure chain of custody.

  • White-glove onsite removal
  • Secure, GPS-tracked transportation
  • Detailed inventory reporting

Logistics Excellence

Our logistics framework is built on accountability. Every asset is tagged and tracked from the moment it leaves your facility until final processing.

Certified Compliance

We provide full documentation for your compliance audits, ensuring your organization meets all regulatory requirements for data privacy.

Secure Data Destruction

We perform certified data destruction on all data-bearing devices. Using both software sanitization and physical shredding, we ensure that no data can ever be recovered. Upon completion, you receive a Certificate of Data Destruction for full compliance and peace of mind.

  • NIST 800-88 & DoD Standards
  • Physical hard drive shredding
  • Witnessed destruction available

Device Refurbishment and Resale

Maximize the value of your retired assets. Eligible devices are professionally refurbished and resold through our strategic channels. We offer revenue sharing back to the client where applicable, helping to offset the costs of decommissioning.

  • Asset remarketing services
  • Component-level recovery
  • Capital recovery programs

Value Recovery

Our remarketing team works to find the best market value for your retired equipment, providing a sustainable way to recover value from aging technology.

Environmental Impact

We partner with certified downstream processors to ensure that every material is handled with the highest environmental standards.

IT Equipment Recycling

For devices that cannot be refurbished, we provide responsible recycling through certified downstream partners. Our Zero Landfill Policy ensures that all materials are broken down into raw components for reuse in manufacturing, minimizing environmental impact.

  • Zero Landfill Policy
  • Certified downstream partners
  • ESG reporting support
